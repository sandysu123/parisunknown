Shopping

Shopping in Paris is the thing to do. From au courant fashion to tres mignon vintage finds, you are guaranteed a good time shopping!

Champ Elysees

Champ Elysees is where all the major shopping takes place. You can find all the designer brands here from Chanel to Prada to Louis Vuitton. Visit some of the lesser known Parisian designers that give you the au courant French look, without having to break the bank. These designers are Repetto, Carel, Sandro, and more! Make sure you bring your passport as they cannot give you a tax refund if you do not bring it!

Flower

What is more Parisian than shopping for flowers? Paris is known for their excellent skills in creating the most beautiful bouquets. One of my favorite shops in Paris is a simple one named, Flower. Located in the hip, St. Germain, you will become mesmerized by this beautiful shop upon entry. Floor to ceiling flowers decorate this store! Plus, the florists are passionate and helpful!

Les Merveilles de Babellou

Paris is known for its ultra high end shopping, but what about its equally, fun sister: vintage shopping? In Les Merveilles de Babellou, you will find anything from vintage rare Chanel pieces to clothing from the 1920s. This store is so fun and there is something for everyone. Return home with a vintage Chanel bag for half the price! Celebrities are also known to frequent this store and you may spot a famous actress browsing!
