About


Bon Jour!

Welcome to my website! I have been to Paris twice in my life. The first time I went to Paris, I visited all the touristy locations. Yes, I waited on the long lines of the Eiffel Tower and waded through the ridiculous level of crowds just to see the tiny little Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci. Forget about how lost I was trying to get out of La Louve!

The second time I went to Paris, it was much more relaxed. After getting all the tourist stuff out of the way, I was able to explore and live Paris. I discovered so many hidden gems in the city and when I came home, I couldn't wait to tell my friends and family all about it!

I created these travel guides from my travels in Paris. I hope that you can also benefit from these curated spots and hidden gems. This isn't where the masses of tourists go. This is where you might find the best baguette or the tastiest macaroons, hanging with the locals.

Bon Voyage!
